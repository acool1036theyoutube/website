Scratch Messaging Extension is now Scratch Addons!

What happened?

The Scratch Messaging Extension is now Scratch Addons. Enjoy your experience of easy reading and replying to your messages, as well as more features to make your Scratch experience better!

What is Scratch Addons?

Scratch Addons is an extension with a mission to collect, archive, and put into use userstyles and userscripts, developed by several members of the Scratch community.

Is there anything that I need to do?

In most cases, nothing. Chances are that you see this page after an extension update, which means that Scratch Addons is already installed. If you have something you need to say about this change, create a new issue here.
