What is Scratch Addons?

Scratch Addons is an extension with a mission to collect, archive, and put into use userstyles and userscripts, developed by several members of the Scratch community.

Features

Various addons to empower your Scratch experience

Easy-to-use interface with various controls for each addons

Free, open source, and maintained by various people from the Scratch community

Install

Chrome Firefox

Not yet, but you may load it locally.
